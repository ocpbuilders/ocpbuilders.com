How Leasing Works with OCP Builds

We don't stop at construction. Our complete leasing and management system is designed specifically for our 4400 sq ft duplexes — helping investors achieve faster lease-up, higher rental income, and truly passive ownership.

The Model

Why Room-by-Room Leasing Works Better

Our duplexes are purpose-built for individual bedroom rentals. This model consistently outperforms traditional whole-unit leasing in three key ways:

💰

Higher Overall Income

Eight individual rental income streams from one property consistently outperform what a single household lease would generate on the same square footage.

🛡️

Lower Vacancy Risk

If one tenant moves out, the other seven stay. You continue collecting rent while filling the single vacancy — instead of losing 100% of unit income like in a traditional rental.

🏡

Stronger Tenant Appeal

Quality tenants who want privacy with community benefits are actively looking for this model — young professionals, relocating workers, and graduate students are a large and growing market in the Phoenix metro.

Step by Step

Our Proven Leasing Process

01
Before You're Even Done Building

Strategic Pre-Leasing Marketing

Marketing begins while the property is nearing completion. We run targeted advertising on Facebook and other platforms with automatic messaging that highlights pricing tiers and room options — standard rooms, balcony suites, premium views, and more. By the time keys are ready, tenants often are too.

02
Protecting Your Property & Community

Thorough Tenant Screening

Every applicant completes our proprietary risk-analysis questionnaire. This points-based system evaluates cleanliness, responsibility, payment history, and lifestyle fit. Only well-qualified tenants move forward — protecting your property and the other residents already in place.

03
Built for Flexibility

Flexible Lease Options

We offer 1-month, 2-month, or 12-month lease terms. Tenants can choose membership-style agreements or traditional leases depending on their needs. This flexibility reduces adversarial turnover and keeps occupancy high.

04
Hands-Off for You

Professional Onboarding & Move-In

We handle all paperwork, key distribution, and initial property orientation. You do not need to be present or involved — we take care of every detail so your first day as a landlord is also your first day of truly passive income.

05
Long-Term Performance

Ongoing Management & Technology Platform

Our custom tenant portal and owner dashboard make everything simple and transparent. You choose the level of involvement that fits your lifestyle — fully hands-off or hybrid oversight.
