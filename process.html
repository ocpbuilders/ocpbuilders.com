From Vision to Cash Flow

Our Process

At OCP Builds, we deliver a seamless, transparent experience from the first conversation to long-term cash flow. One trusted team. No juggling contractors, realtors, or property managers.

01
Where It Starts

Discovery & Strategy Meeting

We begin with a focused, no-pressure meeting — in-person or virtual. We listen to your goals, timeline, budget, and vision. From this conversation, we create a custom project roadmap tailored specifically to you.

Do you want to live in one unit and rent the rest?
Are you looking for fully passive income?
What cash-flow targets and exit strategy do you have?
02
Finding the Right Foundation

Land Acquisition & Due Diligence

Our preferred real estate partners scout and negotiate the best land opportunities in the Phoenix/Tempe metro area and surrounding markets. We evaluate every site thoroughly so you only move forward on properties with strong income potential.

Zoning compatibility for duplex or multi-unit builds
Utilities, flood zones, and infrastructure access
Local rental demand and neighborhood trajectory
03
Planning & Approvals

Design & Permitting

Our in-house and partnered architects design your 4400 sq ft 8×8 duplex — or custom build — optimized for room-by-room leasing. Every plan maximizes natural light, privacy, functionality, and tenant appeal. We handle all architectural submissions, engineering, and permitting so you don't have to chase city approvals.

04
Structuring the Deal

Financing & Investment Structuring

We connect you with trusted mortgage brokers and lenders who understand investment properties. Whether you are using conventional loans, portfolio loans, or creative financing options, we help structure the deal that makes the most sense for your situation.

05
The Build Phase

Construction – Built to Premium Standards

Once permits are secured, our construction team takes over. We complete the full build phase in six months or less, without cutting corners on quality.

Superior materials and craftsmanship throughout
Strict quality control at every phase
Regular progress updates and site visits
Full build completion in six months or less
06
Optional but Recommended

Tenant Placement & Stabilization

For clients who want immediate income, we activate our full tenant acquisition system — built for speed and quality. We don't just fill rooms; we place the right people.

Targeted advertising on Facebook and other platforms
Automated messaging with pricing and tier options — standard, balcony, and premium rooms
Proprietary risk-analysis prescreening questionnaire
Thorough background and credit review
Flexible lease terms — 1-month, 2-month, or 12-month options
Membership or traditional lease agreements
07
