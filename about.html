The Founders

Alonzo & Angelica Cruz

Angelica was born and raised in Arizona. Alonzo grew up between Los Angeles and the Valley. Two different paths that led to the same place — a shared decision to stop working for someone else and start building something of their own.

They invested everything they had into OCP Builds. No safety net, no fallback plan — just a commitment to doing the work right and earning the trust of every client they took on. Five years later, that bet has paid off. OCP has been a part of over 100 builds across the Phoenix metro — some built entirely in-house, others completed alongside trusted partners and subcontractors who share the same standard.

What has never changed is the standard. Every project — whether a signature income duplex, a fully custom home, or a complete remodel — gets the same attention, the same materials, and the same commitment to quality that Alonzo and Angelica built this company on.

What We Do

We Build. We Don't Do Everything.

OCP is not a massive corporation chasing every type of contract. We are a focused build team that does three things and does them well — income-producing duplexes, custom homes, and remodels.

We are not building semiconductor facilities or commercial towers. We build homes — the kind that families live in, investors profit from, and owners are proud of for years to come.

That focus is intentional. It means every person on our team is an expert in exactly what we build. It means our process is refined, our timelines are reliable, and our quality is consistent.
